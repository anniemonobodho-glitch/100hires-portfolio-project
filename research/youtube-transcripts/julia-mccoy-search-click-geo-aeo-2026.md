## Key Takeaways

- Zero-click is the new normal — AI tools like Google's AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity now answer questions without sending users to websites. By end of 2026, 40%+ of searches will result in zero clicks to external sites.
- AEO vs GEO serve different goals — AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) wins fast visibility on specific queries using structured answers, FAQs, and schema markup. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) builds long-term authority so AI systems cite your content when generating answers.
- Use both, not one or the other — AEO for transactional pages (pricing, comparisons, FAQs). GEO for thought leadership and in-depth guides. There's a 12–18 month window right now where early movers can dominate before everyone else catches up.
